Close-Loop in Bioelectronic

近日鲍哲南老师在NBT上发表了一篇关于创面闭环治疗的文章，引起了我的关注。我心想，不如建立一个文件，把与闭环医用电子系统相关的经典文献汇总与此。

1. Wireless, closed-loop, smart bandage with integrated sensors and stimulators for advanced wound care and accelerated healing

发表时间：2022-11-24
发表期刊：Nature Biotechnology
关键词：

1.1. Pointed Issues

  • Incorporate sensing and electrical stimulation technologies
  • random mechanical movement produce uncertainties on stimulation and sensing

Aim of development

  • flexible and wirelessly
  • integrate sensing and stimulation
  • on-demand skin adhesion with easy detachment for signal transduction and energy delivery

1.2. Solution

1.2.1. Wireless circuit

  • an energy-harvesting antenna
  • a microcontroller unit
  • a crystal oscillator and filter circuits for dual-channel continuous sensing of wound impedance and temperature
  • parallel stimulation circuit to deliver programmed electrical cues for accelerated wound healing

1.2.2. Hydrogel interface

  • ionically and electrically conducting
  • thermally controlled peeling off via heat phase transition mechanism

1.3. Results and Discussion

1.3.1. 电路设计

通过阻抗传感器和热传感器获取创面感染和炎症信息

热传感通过RF430FRL152H内部的电路进行传感

阻抗响应先后通过：oscillator发生32.768kHz，分别通过创面和内部标准阻抗元件后，经过高通滤波器滤波，在envelope dector转直流，最后在ADC0转换。

文章在这里还放了一个ADC校准

这里其实有几个问题，1. 为何不使用芯片内部的分频器进行分频；2. 固定的频率（32.768kHz）是否能得到足够的创面阻抗信息

1.3.2 水凝胶界面

由于PEDOT：PSS存在colloidal aqueous suspension, 在离子和碱性条件下发生聚沉，因此开发了一种通过hydrogen peroxide 和 ascorbic acid进行homogeneous gelation的方式

这里关于电化学阻抗的讨论可以学习一下：Compared with the pristine poly(NIPAM-ran-AAm) gel, the incorporation of PEDOT:PSS substantially reduced interfacial impedance when in contact with phosphate buffered saline (PBS), **with a ~0° phase angle across the entire frequency range **(Fig. 3c and Supplementary Fig. 8), corresponding to a resistive nature for the contact due to the high capacitance at the low frequency range for PEDOT:PSS35. Similarly, when a voltage pulse was applied, the PEDOT:PSS gel showed substantially enhanced charge injection capacity when compared with the control sample (Fig. 3d and Supplementary Fig. 9), which ensures efficient delivery of stimulus from the electronically conducting circuits to ionically conducting tissues. The low impedance and high charge injection of the hydrogel electrode can be well maintained even after 10,000 cycles of repetitive charge injections

力学表征
有空详细看看Xuanhe Zhao关于水凝胶力学的讨论

这里使用了unidirectional tensile test, reversible impedance changes (力学-电学关联)，

lower critical solution temperature (LCST) transition: NIPAM的amphiphilic NIPAM units在热力条件下aggregation

hydrophilic monomers 调节LCST points，通过differential scanning calorimetry(DSC)和rheological measurements表征

值得注意的是，还和3M的医用胶带进行了比较

1.3.2 动物实验

基本验证：动物行为学（不会干扰动物运动），生物相容性

观测指标：

  • wound closure duration,
  • wound impedance,
  • tensile recovery（这个似乎是直接用万能试验机进行测量）
  • HE/Masson (dermal thickness, collagen deposion and dermal appendage count)，collagen fiber heterogeneity
  • 通过天狼星红胶原染色和Curve Align, CT-Fire and MatFiber三种标记算法进行分析
  • neovascularization (CD31, α-SMA), microvessel count

1.4. Advances Techniques

  • parabiosis（异种共生）实验用于研究circulating cells

    本篇工作研究了长期电刺激对循环细胞的影响

  • single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq)

    • Trajectories of cells
      本文利用单细胞测序追踪的方式观察了细胞对电流响应的机理（CytoTRACE)
    • RNA velocity analysis
    • 流形分析（之前说的降维方法）
    • Pseudotime analysis

具体过程看原文 Page 6,包括大量生物信息学分析
