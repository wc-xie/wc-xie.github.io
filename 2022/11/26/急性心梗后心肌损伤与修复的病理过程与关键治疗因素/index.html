(Review) 急性心梗后心肌损伤与修复的病理过程与关键治疗因素

(Review) 急性心梗后心肌损伤与修复的病理过程与关键治疗因素

今天系统的学习一下心梗后心肌损伤与修复的病理过程

1. MI病理学

1.1. 心梗的早期组织学特征

1.1.1. Time-dependent myocardical injury

  • seconds：严重的收缩功能下降
  • minutes：心肌细胞超结构改变
  • <15-20 min：心肌细胞的可逆损伤
  • >20 min：细胞不可逆损伤
  • >60 min：大多数梗死灶内膜下心肌细胞死亡

1.1.2. 细胞/组织病理学特征

1.1.2.1. 细胞病理学特征
  • Sarcolemmal disruption
    • Small breaks in the plasmalemma
    • Sormation of subsarcolemmal blebs
  • Striking perturbations of the mitochondrial architecture
    • Mitochondrial swelling
    • Disorganized cristae (线粒体嵴)
    • Ultrastructural evidence of amorphous matrix densities
1.1.2.2. 组织病理学特征

[早期]

  • Karyolysis
  • vacuolar degeneration (myocytolysis)
  • hypereosinophilia

[中期]

  • “Wavy” (elongated and narrow) fibers
  • interstitial edema
  • leukocytes infiltration

[早期再灌注]

  • Reactive Hyperemia
  • Extravasation of blood cells
  • Contraction bands of hypercontracted sarcomeres
  • Prominent subsarcolemmal blebs and granular mitochondrial densities
1.1.2.3. 心肌坏死的时空过程

对于狗：坏死从心内膜->中->外膜发展
对于鼠和羊：mid-myocardial is rather vulnerable

2. 细胞死亡机制

Apoptosis与Necrosis共同参与了梗死灶中心肌细胞的死亡

  • 缺血期间，大部分心肌细胞发生Necrosis

  • 再灌注期间，大部分心肌细胞通过Apoptosis死亡

  • First 24 h: 大部分心肌死亡

  • Inflammatory and biomechanical stress导致第二轮细胞死亡

3. 心肌细胞死亡分子机制

3.1. Apoptosis

3.1.1. Permeabilization of the Outer mitochondrial membrane (OMM)

3.1.1.1. B-cell lymphoma 2 (Bcl-2) family

[Classification]

  1. The antiapoptotic members
    • Bcl-2
    • Bcl-extra large (bcl-Xl)
    • Mcl-1 that stabilize the OMM
  2. Multidomain proapoptotic members that permeabilize the OMM
    • bcl-2 associated X protein (Bax)
    • Bcl-2 homologous antagonist/killer (Bak)
  3. BH3-only proapoptotic proteins
    • Bad, Bnip3, Nix, Bid, Puma
    • act as Bax activators by inducing conformational changes through direct binding
    • or sensitizers by displacing antiapoptotic members

[Function]

Proapoptotic members deficiency or antiapoptotic overexpress helped to scar size reduction

3.1.1.2. Release of mitochondrial apoptogens

Cyrochrome c binds to the adaptor protein apoptotic protease activating factor 1 (Apaf-1), causing its oligomerization and mediating recruitment of procaspase-9.

Caspase-9 induces downstream activation of procaspases-3 and -7 that catalyze cleavage of many cell proteins causing apoptotic death.

3.2. Necrosis

necrosis is defined by opening of the mitochondrial permeability transition pore (mPTP), a pore in the inner mitochondrial membranes (IMM)

[Consequence]

  1. Ca2+ entry into the mitochondria triggers opening of the mPTP
  2. loss of mitochondrial membrane potential and cessation of ATP synthesis
  3. influx of water and mitochondrial swelling.
  4. OMM rupture

3.3. Autophagy

Autophagy defined by Dysfunctional or unnecessary components of the cell are delivered to lysosomes for degradation.

MI induces autophagy mediated through an AMP-activated protein kinase (AMPK)-dependent pathway, protects cardiomyocytes from death, but has protective and deleterious effects.

3.4. Noncardiomyocytes

  • Fibroblasts
  • Vascular cells
    • Endothelial cells
    • Pericytes
    • Vascular smooth muscle cells
  • Inflammatory cells
    • macrophage
    • dendritic cells
    • mast cells

4. Cardiac Repair

As the infarcted area heals, the ventricle dilates to maintain cardiac output and the noninfarcted segments undergo extensive remodeling due to pressure and volume overload

4.1. Inflammatory Phase

characterized by activation of innate immune pathways, recruitment of inflammatory leukocytes that clear the wound from dead cells

4.1.1. The alarmins

The danger signals released from intracellular content and activate innate immune.

toll-like receptors (TLRs),

4.1.1.1. Categories

[Intercellular]

  • High mobility group box 1 (HMGB1)
    mediated through activation of receptor for advanced glycation end-products (RAGE)
  • S100 family
  • interleukin (IL)-1a
  • ATP
  • RNA
  • heat-shock protein

[Extracellular]

  • hyaluronan
  • fibronectin fragments
4.1.1.2. Proinflammatory pathway
  • TLR
    • TLR2, TLR4
  • interferon-β-mediated transcription factor (Trif)
    activated by TLR3 + extracellular RNA
  • RAGE
  • IL-receptor dependent pathways
4.1.1.3. Complement System

补体系统激活加重细胞缓释，并直接导致炎症反应

C3 fragmentation products promote leukocyte chemoattraction.

C5 对炎症过程也同样有作用

但目前临床试验没有效果

4.1.1.4. ROS Reaction
  • ROS generation activates immune cells in the infarcted heart.
  • ROS promote leukocyte chemotaxis
    • activating complement
    • stimulating expression of P-selectin
    • activation of the nuclear factor (NF)-κB system to upregulating cytokine and chemokine synthesis through
  • ROS promote cell apoptosis and
  • ROS induce degradation of the extracellular matrix

ROS既在炎症phase有破坏作用，也在后期起到修复作用
free radical scavengers in MI had both protective and detrimental effects

[ROS Scaveraging]

  • Enzymatic Pathway
    • catalase
    • glutathione peroxidase
    • superoxide dismutases
  • intracellular antioxidants
4.1.1.4.1. NF-kB System

[激活信号]

  • TLR signaling
  • IL-1 signaling
  • RAGE-dependent signaling

[激活过程]
正常细胞质含有：inhibitors of κB(IκB)

激活信号通路磷酸化/泛素化IκB，释放NF-κB

[下游信号]
proinflammatory cytokines
NF-kB同样具有双重作用

4.1.1.5. Cytokine Signaling
  • IL-1
    介导炎性反应和细胞浸润
    • induction of endothelial adhesion molecules
    • synthesis of chemotactic mediators
    • stimulating leukocyte recruitment
    • induces a matrix-degrading phenotype
    • inhibiting myofibroblast transdifferentiation
    • suppressing fibroblast proliferation
    • delay activation of a profibrotic program of fibroblasts
  • TNF-α
    pleiotropic effect
  • IL-6 family
    • receptor is shared in gp130 family
    • act through JAK/STAT pathway
4.1.1.6. Chemokines
CC chemokines CXC chemokines with ELR CXC w/o ELR
Example monocyte chemoattractant protein (MCP)-1/CCL2 (IL-8/CXCL8) interferon-γ-inducible protein (IP)-10/CXCL10
Function attract monocytes attract neutrophil antifibrotic and angiostatic actions

ELR: (GluLeu-Arg) tripeptide sequence

4.1.1.7. Cellular effectors

[Cardiomyocytes]

Necrotic cardiomyocytes release signals to activate inflammatory cascades in fibroblasts, immune, and vascular cells.

border zone cardiomyocytes release IL-6, and IL-1β from inflammasome.

[Fibroblast]

Also releases proinflammatory cytokines and chemokines after infarction.

[Endothelial]

a major source of proinflammatory chemokines (such as CCL2 and CXCL10)

[Platelets]

Contribute to the fibrinbased provisional matrix for blood-derived cells and reparative mesenchymal cells.

An important source of transforming growth factor-β (TGF-β) and contributed to the development of hypertrophic remodeling.

[Neutrophils]

Contribute to clearance of dead cells and matrix debris by generating ROS and by releasing proteolytic enzymes.

[Monocytes and Macrophages]

  • Nonreperfused infarction

    Resident cardiac macrophages are replaced by cells derived from circulating monocytes, originating from bone marrow cells or alternative extramedullary sources

  • Reperfused infarction

    • CC chemokine CCL2 and TGF-β recruit monocytes
    • monocytes activates fibroblasts and endothelial cells

(Function in MI)

  • Phagocytose dead cells and matrix debris and clear the infarct from apoptotic neutrophils and cardiomyocytes
  • Source of cytokines and growth factors regulating inflammatory activity, fibroblast growth and activation, and neovessel formation and maturation
  • Producing matrix metalloproteinases (MMPs) and their inhibitors to remodel extracellular matrix
  • Facilitate formation of new myocardium to promote myocardial regeneration

[Mast cell]

通过释放 histamine and TNF-α 促进炎症，但作用大小未知

[Lymphocytes]

4.1.2. Extracellular matrix

4.1.2.1. MMP activation

MMP在心梗10分钟后即激活

15-30 min可在心肌内观察到collagen fragment

透明质酸也可被切断，这些碎片产生pro-inflammation effect

4.1.2.2. MMP regulate inflammation

MMP 同样能作用于TNF-a, IL-1, TGF-β，通过产生无作用的片段产生免疫block作用

4.1.2.3. Matrix fragments activate inflamation

Collagen and elastin fragments 刺激中性粒细胞，单核细胞和fibroblast

Hyaluronan fragments 刺激内皮细胞和巨噬细胞

4.1.2.4. Provisional extracellular matrix

梗死心肌胶原的降解伴随着新的fibrinbased matrix的沉积

血浆中fibrinogen 与 fibronectin 构建了MI位置动态的matrix

作为浸润的白细胞、成纤维细胞和血管细胞的桥梁

细胞表面的integrins与fibrin/fibronectin的相互作用改变细胞表型

4.2. Proliferative Phage

associated with suppression of proinflammatory signaling and infiltration of the infarct with mesenchymal cells that secrete matrix proteins

[Process]

  1. Neutrophils apoptosis
  2. Clearance of proinflammatory leukocyte
  3. Myofibroblasts and vascular cells activate
  4. Macrophage and lymphocytes secret cytokines and growth factors
  5. Extracellular matrix undergo dynamic alterations

4.2.1. Suppression of inflammation

resolution of inflammation不仅仅是坏死细胞清除，还存在一系列抑炎通路激活

4.2.2. Cell Biology of Suppression

残骸清除是修复过程中最重要的过程

细胞吞噬过程同时会释放抗炎因子（IL-10, TGF-β) 并促进脂质分解（lipoxins, resolvins)

凋亡的中性粒细胞阻止进一步的recruitment (Annexin A1, lactoferrin)

M1/M2 细胞是最重要的调节细胞群，介导抗炎，纤维化和血管化

Invariant NKT (iNKT) cells and regulatory T cells (Tregs) 是抑制性cytokines的source

Hyaluronan 通过与CD44相互作用减轻炎症。

4.2.3. Molecular Signals implicated in suppression

4.2.3.1. Downregulation of TLR signal

由于TLR起促炎和损伤作用，因此下调TLR通路是新的作用方式

Interleukin-1 Receptor Associated Kinase (IRAK)是一种无激酶活性的受体，是MI炎症中的关键停止信号

其通路中还有：TAM receptor protein tyrosine kinases， receptor interacting protein 3 (RIP3), activating transcription factor 3, the autophagy-related molecule Atg16L1, the TLR-inducible zinc finger proteins A20 and Zc3h12a

促进IL-10， TGF-β的合成 或 诱导decoy receptors 都能起到终止炎症的作用

4.2.3.2. IL-10

IL 10 produces by T cells and monocytes/macrophage

monocytes responsive to IL-10

IL-10 is mainly role in acute but no in resolution phase

4.2.3.3. TGF-β

Border Zone 区域 Smad下调

TGF-β 介导抑炎和修复作用

4.2.3.4. Lipid Mediators

the lipoxins, resolvins, protectins, and the maresins

4.2.4. Fibroblast activation

死亡的细胞清除后，fibroblasts 转分化为 myofibroblast

[Characteristic]

  1. Increased synthesis of both structural and matricellular extracellular matrix proteins

  2. A proliferative phenotype

  3. Expression of contractile proteins, such as α-smooth muscle actin (SMA), and acquisition of a myofibroblast phenotype

[Source]

间质中的成纤维细胞是myocardium中的重要细胞来源

血原性的fibroblast 和 endothelial cells, epicardial cells, cardiac pericytes, and vascular smooth muscle cells 同样发生 mesenchymal transition

[Activator]

  • Angiotensin II
    a-SMA可用于鉴定myofibroblasts, 在BZ区可以发现contractile stress fibers
  • Growth factors
    • TGF-β
  • Matricellular proteins
    • ED-A fibronectin

TGF-β induces extracellular matrix protein synthesis and stimulates protease inhibitors

cononical pathway (Smad involved) and noncanonical (p38 mitogen, c-Abl tyrosine kinase) cascades participate in TGF-β fibroblast activation.

Smad3 activates matrix protein synthesis and stimulates a-SMA expression

[Neurohomoral Pathway]

  • Angiotensin II

Ang II激活myofibroblasts上的AT1受体发挥基质合成功能

  • Aldosterone

direct stimulation of cardiac fibroblasts
or activation of a profibrotic program in other cell

Other fibrogenic mediators ↓

  • latelet-derived growth factor (PDGF),
  • fibroblast growth factors
  • the mast cell proteases tryptase and chymase

all stimulate fibroblast proliferation and induce of matrix protein synthesis

4.2.5. ECM in infarct healing

Matricellular protein 不仅作为结构，还发挥多种细胞作用

  • TSP-1
    TGF-β activator and MMP inhibitor, with angiostatic and anti-inflammatory properties
    upregulated in BZ

  • tenascin-C

    promotes a deadhesive state
    facilitate migration of fibroblasts

  • TSP-2

    maintaining the structural integrity of the healing infarct

  • Osteopontin

    reflect differentiation and activation of infiltrating monocytes
    play a protective role in the remodeling infarcted heart

  • SPARC

    Function in organization of the scar and for deposition of mature collagen

  • Periostin

    Stimulates fibroblast activation and recruitment in the scar through FAK/integrindependent signaling

4.2.6. Angiogenesis

infarct angiogenesis primarily functions to provide oxygen and nutrients to the metabolically active mesenchymal cells in the healing infarct

Angiogenesis pathway is activated within few hours of ischemia

[Formation]

  1. 早期：新生血管缺乏pericyte，高透，支持炎症细胞迁移
  2. 瘢痕形成后：血管开始成熟，稳定创伤组织
  3. 一部分血管壁细胞形成，一部分血管凋亡
  4. Pericyte 包覆的血管通透性降低，并减少新的angiogenesis

Mural cells 与endothelial cells 通过PDGF-BB-PDGFR-β通路相互作用

Pericyte-coted vessel 是MI后肉芽组织形成的关键步骤，促进炎症消退和疤痕稳定

4.3. Maturation

Associated with quiescence and removal of the reparative cells and cross-linking of the matrix

Structural Matrix 的形成标志着MI后修复期的终止与Maturation Phase的开始

[Characteristic]

  1. Collagen is crosslinked through upregulation of enzymes such as lysyloxidase
  2. Expression of matricellular proteins decreases
  3. the number of activated myofibroblasts in the infarct is markedly reduced and undergo apoptosis/suppressed matrix-synthetic capacity/attenuated expression of contractile proteins
  4. Angiogenesis is inhibited and uncoated infarct microvessels regress

5. Ventricular Remodeling

  • The chamber dilates and becomes more spherical
    Chamber dilation is associated with progression of systolic dysfunction, increased mortality, a higher incidence of arrhythmias
  • The infarcted segments become thinner
  • The viable myocardial segments undergo hypertrophy
    Overload of the noninfarcted myocardium leading to hypertrophy and fibrosis

5.1. Infarct expansion and ventricular dilation

[Definition]

Cardiomyocyte slippage and matrix degradation due to protease activation have been implicated in the pathogenesis of infarct expansion

[Processes]

  1. Rearrangement of cardiomyocyte bundles accompanied by elongation of cardiomyocytes.
    Reduce the number of layers across the ventricular wall
  2. Degradation of the interstitial matrix through activation of MMPs.
    Promote chamber dilation contributing to the expansion process
  3. Alterations in the organization, spatial polarization, and contraction of infarct myofibroblasts
    Result in perturbed scar maturation enhancing chamber dilation

5.2. Ventricular aneurysms

[Definition]

Dyskinetic area of the infarcted left ventricular wall with a broad neck

无收缩力的瘢痕组织在压力下膨出

心衰、血栓栓塞和心动过速的概率高

6. Complications associated with MI

6.1. Cardiac rupture

[Part]

  • Papillary muscle rupture
    causing acute mitral regurgitation
  • Interventricular septum
    causing a ventricular septal defect
  • Left ventricular free wall
    causing hemopericardium and cardiac tamponade

[Factors]

  • Large transmural infarction
  • Accentuated inflammatory
  • Delayed reparative response
  • Activation of matrix-degrading proteases

早期再灌注有效降低Cardiac Rupture的发生

6.2. Cardiogenic shock

[Morbidity]
5% to 8% of patients hospitalized with STEMI
2% to 3% of patients with non-STEMI

[Definition]

a state of hypoperfusion resulting from a low cardiac output due to heart failure

Cardiogenic shock remains a major cause of early mortality following MI

[Pathophysiology]

Vascular alternation:

  • Catecholamine release and increased angiotensin II levels
    血管收缩血压升高
  • 后负荷升高，水钠潴留
    肺水肿加重

Neurohormonal Changes:

  • Catecholamine 导致心动过速，增加氧耗，恶化 ischemia

Immunology Alternation:

  • 心源性休克导致炎症因子飙升

有趣的是 心肌损伤的程度 与发生 心源性休克 之间的关系没有想象中的大
即使发生了心源性休克，患者依然有可能完全康复

6.3. Arrhythmogenesis and conduction defects

[Process]

Early: Ionic alterations caused by ischemia creates substants that generate arrhythmias.

Later: Fibrosis creates macroscopic discontinuities in conduction, and generating inexcitable barriers and reentry circuits.

[Relative Factors]

  • 交感神经激活提高Purkinje fibers兴奋性
  • 区域性的肾上腺素神经支配（这个有点奇怪，值得深入探讨）
  • RAAS通过离子通道、促炎和纤维化通路导致心律失常
  • 氧化应激改变钠离子通道

研究发现 坏死区域apical端的 非坏死区域 发生了 异质性交感神经去神经支配

6.4. Pericardial disease

[Morbidity]

MI后心包炎的发病率高达7%到41%

Pericarditis 多见于透壁性MI，源于梗死区的炎性反应

postmyocardial infarction syndrome” (Dressler syndrome)
characterized by + chest pain, + malaise, + fever, + evidence of systemic inflammation (leukocytosis, elevated erythrocyte sedimentation rate), and + development of fibrinous pericarditis

Autoimmunological reaction

7. Pathophysiologic Basis of Treatment in MI

7.1. β-Blockers

通过降低心率、血压和发挥负性肌力作用减少心肌氧耗

减弱神经和体液交感神经作用，减少MI后心律失常

7.2. ACEI

通过降低血压，改善血流动力学，调整神经内分泌改善remodelling

改善离子流减少心律失常

抑制炎症和凋亡

7.3. Statins

抗炎、促进凝血、促进纤溶系统

7.4. Future therapeutic strategies

several promising therapeutic approaches and targets

  • ischemic postconditioning
  • inhibition of oxidative stress
  • attenuation of calcium overload
  • cyclosporine
  • correction of pH
  • suppression of proapoptotic pathways
  • activation of prosurvival pathways

8. Outlook

  • Which molecular signals regulate cell death in the infarcted heart and + how can we interfere to reduce cardiomyocyte susceptibility to ischemic + injury?
  • Which cells and molecular pathways govern repair of the infarcted heart?
  • Can we enhance the regenerative capacity of cardiomyocytes?
  • What is the role and fate of the abundant populations of interstitial cells in the heart and how do they affect cardiac function?
  • Which pathophysiologic mechanisms make certain patient subpopulations more susceptible to dysrhythmias, complications or heart failure following infarction?
