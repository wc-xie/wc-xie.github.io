(Review) 急性心梗后心肌损伤与修复的病理过程与关键治疗因素

(Review) 急性心梗后心肌损伤与修复的病理过程与关键治疗因素

今天系统的学习一下心梗后心肌损伤与修复的病理过程

MI病理学

心梗的早期组织学特征

Time-dependent myocardical injury

  • seconds：严重的收缩功能下降
  • minutes：心肌细胞超结构改变
  • <15-20 min：心肌细胞的可逆损伤
  • >20 min：细胞不可逆损伤
  • >60 min：大多数梗死灶内膜下心肌细胞死亡

细胞/组织病理学特征

细胞病理学特征
  • Sarcolemmal disruption
    • Small breaks in the plasmalemma
    • Sormation of subsarcolemmal blebs
  • Striking perturbations of the mitochondrial architecture
    • Mitochondrial swelling
    • Disorganized cristae (线粒体嵴)
    • Ultrastructural evidence of amorphous matrix densities
组织病理学特征

[早期]

  • Karyolysis
  • vacuolar degeneration (myocytolysis)
  • hypereosinophilia

[中期]

  • “Wavy” (elongated and narrow) fibers
  • interstitial edema
  • leukocytes infiltration

[早期再灌注]

  • Reactive Hyperemia
  • Extravasation of blood cells
  • Contraction bands of hypercontracted sarcomeres
  • Prominent subsarcolemmal blebs and granular mitochondrial densities
心肌坏死的时空过程

对于狗：坏死从心内膜->中->外膜发展
对于鼠和羊：mid-myocardial is rather vulnerable

细胞死亡机制

Apoptosis与Necrosis共同参与了梗死灶中心肌细胞的死亡

  • 缺血期间，大部分心肌细胞发生Necrosis

  • 再灌注期间，大部分心肌细胞通过Apoptosis死亡

  • First 24 h: 大部分心肌死亡

  • Inflammatory and biomechanical stress导致第二轮细胞死亡

心肌细胞死亡分子机制

Apoptosis

Permeabilization of the Outer mitochondrial membrane (OMM)

B-cell lymphoma 2 (Bcl-2) family

[Classification]

  1. The antiapoptotic members
    • Bcl-2
    • Bcl-extra large (bcl-Xl)
    • Mcl-1 that stabilize the OMM
  2. Multidomain proapoptotic members that permeabilize the OMM
    • bcl-2 associated X protein (Bax)
    • Bcl-2 homologous antagonist/killer (Bak)
  3. BH3-only proapoptotic proteins
    • Bad, Bnip3, Nix, Bid, Puma
    • act as Bax activators by inducing conformational changes through direct binding
    • or sensitizers by displacing antiapoptotic members

[Function]

Proapoptotic members deficiency or antiapoptotic overexpress helped to scar size reduction

Release of mitochondrial apoptogens

Cyrochrome c binds to the adaptor protein apoptotic protease activating factor 1 (Apaf-1), causing its oligomerization and mediating recruitment of procaspase-9.

Caspase-9 induces downstream activation of procaspases-3 and -7 that catalyze cleavage of many cell proteins causing apoptotic death.

Necrosis

necrosis is defined by opening of the mitochondrial permeability transition pore (mPTP), a pore in the inner mitochondrial membranes (IMM)

[Consequence]

  1. Ca2+ entry into the mitochondria triggers opening of the mPTP
  2. loss of mitochondrial membrane potential and cessation of ATP synthesis
  3. influx of water and mitochondrial swelling.
  4. OMM rupture

Autophagy

Autophagy defined by Dysfunctional or unnecessary components of the cell are delivered to lysosomes for degradation.

MI induces autophagy mediated through an AMP-activated protein kinase (AMPK)-dependent pathway, protects cardiomyocytes from death, but has protective and deleterious effects.

Noncardiomyocytes

  • Fibroblasts
  • Vascular cells
    • Endothelial cells
    • Pericytes
    • Vascular smooth muscle cells
  • Inflammatory cells
    • macrophage
    • dendritic cells
    • mast cells

Cardiac Repair

As the infarcted area heals, the ventricle dilates to maintain cardiac output and the noninfarcted segments undergo extensive remodeling due to pressure and volume overload

Inflammatory Phase

characterized by activation of innate immune pathways, recruitment of inflammatory leukocytes that clear the wound from dead cells

The alarmins

The danger signals released from intracellular content and activate innate immune.

toll-like receptors (TLRs),

Categories

[Intercellular]

  • High mobility group box 1 (HMGB1)
    mediated through activation of receptor for advanced glycation end-products (RAGE)
  • S100 family
  • interleukin (IL)-1a
  • ATP
  • RNA
  • heat-shock protein

[Extracellular]

  • hyaluronan
  • fibronectin fragments
Proinflammatory pathway
  • TLR
    • TLR2, TLR4
  • interferon-β-mediated transcription factor (Trif)
    activated by TLR3 + extracellular RNA
  • RAGE
  • IL-receptor dependent pathways
Complement System

补体系统激活加重细胞缓释，并直接导致炎症反应

C3 fragmentation products promote leukocyte chemoattraction.

C5 对炎症过程也同样有作用

但目前临床试验没有效果

ROS Reaction
  • ROS generation activates immune cells in the infarcted heart.
  • ROS promote leukocyte chemotaxis
    • activating complement
    • stimulating expression of P-selectin
    • activation of the nuclear factor (NF)-κB system to upregulating cytokine and chemokine synthesis through
  • ROS promote cell apoptosis and
  • ROS induce degradation of the extracellular matrix

ROS既在炎症phase有破坏作用，也在后期起到修复作用
free radical scavengers in MI had both protective and detrimental effects

[ROS Scaveraging]

  • Enzymatic Pathway
    • catalase
    • glutathione peroxidase
    • superoxide dismutases
  • intracellular antioxidants
NF-kB System

[激活信号]

  • TLR signaling
  • IL-1 signaling
  • RAGE-dependent signaling

[激活过程]
正常细胞质含有：inhibitors of κB(IκB)

激活信号通路磷酸化/泛素化IκB，释放NF-κB

[下游信号]
proinflammatory cytokines
NF-kB同样具有双重作用

Cytokine Signaling
  • IL-1
    介导炎性反应和细胞浸润
    • induction of endothelial adhesion molecules
    • synthesis of chemotactic mediators
    • stimulating leukocyte recruitment
    • induces a matrix-degrading phenotype
    • inhibiting myofibroblast transdifferentiation
    • suppressing fibroblast proliferation
    • delay activation of a profibrotic program of fibroblasts
  • TNF-α
    pleiotropic effect
  • IL-6 family
    • receptor is shared in gp130 family
    • act through JAK/STAT pathway
Chemokines
CC chemokines CXC chemokines with ELR CXC w/o ELR
Example monocyte chemoattractant protein (MCP)-1/CCL2 (IL-8/CXCL8) interferon-γ-inducible protein (IP)-10/CXCL10
Function attract monocytes attract neutrophil antifibrotic and angiostatic actions

ELR: (GluLeu-Arg) tripeptide sequence

Cellular effectors

[Cardiomyocytes]

Necrotic cardiomyocytes release signals to activate inflammatory cascades in fibroblasts, immune, and vascular cells.

border zone cardiomyocytes release IL-6, and IL-1β from inflammasome.

[Fibroblast]

Also releases proinflammatory cytokines and chemokines after infarction.

[Endothelial]

a major source of proinflammatory chemokines (such as CCL2 and CXCL10)

[Platelets]

Contribute to the fibrinbased provisional matrix for blood-derived cells and reparative mesenchymal cells.

An important source of transforming growth factor-β (TGF-β) and contributed to the development of hypertrophic remodeling.

[Neutrophils]

Contribute to clearance of dead cells and matrix debris by generating ROS and by releasing proteolytic enzymes.

[Monocytes and Macrophages]

  • Nonreperfused infarction

    Resident cardiac macrophages are replaced by cells derived from circulating monocytes, originating from bone marrow cells or alternative extramedullary sources

  • Reperfused infarction

    • CC chemokine CCL2 and TGF-β recruit monocytes
    • monocytes activates fibroblasts and endothelial cells

(Function in MI)

  • Phagocytose dead cells and matrix debris and clear the infarct from apoptotic neutrophils and cardiomyocytes
  • Source of cytokines and growth factors regulating inflammatory activity, fibroblast growth and activation, and neovessel formation and maturation
  • Producing matrix metalloproteinases (MMPs) and their inhibitors to remodel extracellular matrix
  • Facilitate formation of new myocardium to promote myocardial regeneration

[Mast cell]

通过释放 histamine and TNF-α 促进炎症，但作用大小未知

[Lymphocytes]

Extracellular matrix

MMP activation

MMP在心梗10分钟后即激活

15-30 min可在心肌内观察到collagen fragment

透明质酸也可被切断，这些碎片产生pro-inflammation effect

MMP regulate inflammation

MMP 同样能作用于TNF-a, IL-1, TGF-β，通过产生无作用的片段产生免疫block作用

Matrix fragments activate inflamation

Collagen and elastin fragments 刺激中性粒细胞，单核细胞和fibroblast

Hyaluronan fragments 刺激内皮细胞和巨噬细胞

Provisional extracellular matrix

梗死心肌胶原的降解伴随着新的fibrinbased matrix的沉积

血浆中fibrinogen 与 fibronectin 构建了MI位置动态的matrix

作为浸润的白细胞、成纤维细胞和血管细胞的桥梁

细胞表面的integrins与fibrin/fibronectin的相互作用改变细胞表型

Proliferative Phage

associated with suppression of proinflammatory signaling and infiltration of the infarct with mesenchymal cells that secrete matrix proteins

[Process]

  1. Neutrophils apoptosis
  2. Clearance of proinflammatory leukocyte
  3. Myofibroblasts and vascular cells activate
  4. Macrophage and lymphocytes secret cytokines and growth factors
  5. Extracellular matrix undergo dynamic alterations

Suppression of inflammation

resolution of inflammation不仅仅是坏死细胞清除，还存在一系列抑炎通路激活

Cell Biology of Suppression

残骸清除是修复过程中最重要的过程

细胞吞噬过程同时会释放抗炎因子（IL-10, TGF-β) 并促进脂质分解（lipoxins, resolvins)

凋亡的中性粒细胞阻止进一步的recruitment (Annexin A1, lactoferrin)

M1/M2 细胞是最重要的调节细胞群，介导抗炎，纤维化和血管化

Invariant NKT (iNKT) cells and regulatory T cells (Tregs) 是抑制性cytokines的source

Hyaluronan 通过与CD44相互作用减轻炎症。

Molecular Signals implicated in suppression

Downregulation of TLR signal

由于TLR起促炎和损伤作用，因此下调TLR通路是新的作用方式

Interleukin-1 Receptor Associated Kinase (IRAK)是一种无激酶活性的受体，是MI炎症中的关键停止信号

其通路中还有：TAM receptor protein tyrosine kinases， receptor interacting protein 3 (RIP3), activating transcription factor 3, the autophagy-related molecule Atg16L1, the TLR-inducible zinc finger proteins A20 and Zc3h12a

促进IL-10， TGF-β的合成 或 诱导decoy receptors 都能起到终止炎症的作用

IL-10

IL 10 produces by T cells and monocytes/macrophage

monocytes responsive to IL-10

IL-10 is mainly role in acute but no in resolution phase

TGF-β

Border Zone 区域 Smad下调

TGF-β 介导抑炎和修复作用

Lipid Mediators

the lipoxins, resolvins, protectins, and the maresins

Fibroblast activation

死亡的细胞清除后，fibroblasts 转分化为 myofibroblast

[Characteristic]

  1. Increased synthesis of both structural and matricellular extracellular matrix proteins

  2. A proliferative phenotype

  3. Expression of contractile proteins, such as α-smooth muscle actin (SMA), and acquisition of a myofibroblast phenotype

间质中的成纤维细胞是myocardium中的重要细胞来源

血原性的fibroblast 和 endothelial cells, epicardial cells, cardiac pericytes, and vascular smooth muscle cells 同样发生 mesenchymal transition

Maturation

associated with quiescence and removal of the reparative cells and cross-linking of the matrix
